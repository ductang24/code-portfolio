Hi! I am Tang, a digital design graduate from Multidisciplinary Design Department in Yokohama. I specialize in UI design and Design research. Currently, I'm attending a UI/UX Design course (expected to complete in December.2019) at Minnesota University (US) to achieve a higher understanding in user side.

Based on education and projects' experience, I describe myself as a person who has a passion and required knowledge in Design research. Therefore, on March-2019, I was awarded the Headmaster's Award with a project in the philosophy of design, named "The Lifetime of Homo Sapiens" in the graduation exhibition.

I am proficient in 3 languages (English, Japanese, Vietnamese), so there's no enormous adversity in communication. I also have more than 2 years' experience in merchandise inspection, assisting inventory management and staff supervision as a part-time job. Thanks to this opportunity, I have developed some valuable skills, including Attention to detail and Following strict standards.
